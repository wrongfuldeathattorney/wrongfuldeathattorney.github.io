Wrongful Death Claim

Depending on the State deceased lives, Wrongful death Claims are handled differently. But these are considered in same way under Jurisdiction.

  1. Deep investigations are held in presence of Correct plaintiff. This involves determining the reasons for Wrongful death along with liable person.
  2. On successful investigation of the matter, a demand is made to accused party by Legal Counsel.
  3. Demand presented by Correct plaintiff and Legal Counsel involves agreement for seeking compensation instead of filing Wrongful Death Case.
  4. Other Cases may undergo Litigation.

Once the correct plaintiff is established, the early days of a claim typically involve a thorough investigation. Determining the cause of the wrongful death as well as the responsible parties is vital. Once the investigation is complete, the plaintiff and their legal counsel will issue a demand to the at-fault party. This demand seeks compensation in exchange for an agreement to not file a wrongful death case.

Some wrongful death cases settle at this point. Others will ultimately result in legal action. If a plaintiff files a wrongful death claim, it can take months or even years before a jury trial finally occurs.


Who gets the money in a Wrongful Death Lawsuit?

Under Wrongful Death claim, the compensation is provided to primary beneficiary which includes Spouse, Children and victim's parents. Primary beneficiary are awareded the highest share of Compensation.

On the other hand, the individuals who relied on deceased for Financial needs & Emotional support are entiled as Secondary Benefciary.
